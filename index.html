Hello World. My name is Ryan.




I was born and raised in Mobile, AL. I went to Baker High School, after which I attended the University of South Alabama. During my time at USA, I earned a Bachelor's degree in History with a minor in German. For the past eleven years, I have worked in the petrochemical industry. I spent four years as an API-570 Inspector, and the last seven years as an Operator. As an Operator, part of my job entails controlling units from a console. While working on the console, I have spent a great deal of time with trending and data technologies. I have created numerous historical trends and real-time dashboards using OSI PI and Yokogawa DCS. I have worked alongside Process Controls Engineers and Process Engineers to interpret this data to develop money and time saving solutions for my company. In addition, I have trained in the functions, specifications, operation and troubleshooting aspects of 10 different operating units. I have an American Petroleum Institute certification in piping inspection, and numerous certifications pertaining to refinery operations such as Advanced Hydrotreating and Hydrocracking, and Advanced Hydrogen Reforming.

To further my knowledge of IT and Web Development, I also earned a Google Support Professional Certificate, as well as attended The Web Developer Bootcamp on Udemy.com.

In my spare time, I like to build computers, design web applications and fly planes in the simulator game, Digital Combat Simulator World. I even design my own custom paint schemes for my planes! Please continue to scroll through my portfolio and see my work!
